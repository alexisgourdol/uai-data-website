The uai Methodology

1. Identify Business Needs

We start by clearly identifying tasks that are good candidates for AI Agents to perform, i.e. repetitive, time-consuming and boring tasks, actions performed on jobs where hiring is challenging, , actions where LLMs can empower employees to focus on high-value missions.

2. Technical Assessment

We analyze your technical requirements, including data sources, infrastructure setup, and integration with existing systems such as internal APIs - keeping in mind how to keep the set up simple and cost-effective.

3. Solution Design

Based on the assessment, we design AI solutions that perfectly match your organization's needs and capabilities.

We use open-source libraries that standardize the inputs and outputs of the LLMs, so that it can be integrated easily with your existing systems.
